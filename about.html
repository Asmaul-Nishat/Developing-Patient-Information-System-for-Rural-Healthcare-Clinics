About Our Healthcare Project

Our Mission

Our Rural Healthcare Information System is designed to provide accessible, affordable, and reliable healthcare support for people in rural areas. We aim to bridge the gap between urban and rural medical facilities by implementing digital health solutions such as patient records, telemedicine, and doctor-patient communication.

Objectives

1. Digitize patient healthcare information securely.
2. Provide telemedicine services for remote areas.
3. Support healthcare workers with decision-making tools.
4. Improve access to quality healthcare in rural communities.
